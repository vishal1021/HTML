Marquee

- Used to scroll an element towards left or right or we can say horizontally across or vertically down. - Direction attribute us used inside marquee for direction towards right,left,up,down. - behavior attribute is used to give some properties to marquee for example alternate. - onmouseover="stop();" is to star and onmouseout="stop();" is to stop on hover on to marquee. - scrollamount attribute is for speed.
